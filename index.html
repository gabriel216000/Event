What is WebSwitch?

WebSwitch is an annual public speaking event hosted by the icelandic web development industry. This event is for everyone who is new to the industry or simply curious about it. The main purpose of WebSwitch is to generate interest in web development and empower people to make connections within the icelandic tech community.
